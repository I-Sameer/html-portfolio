My Top Three movies

3 idiots

3 Idiots (2009): Three engineering students, Farhan, Raju, and Rancho, challenge the rigid educational system at a prestigious Indian university. Their innovative approach and emphasis on practical learning clash with the dean's methods, leading to a comedic and inspiring battle for educational reform in India.

taare zameen par

Ishaan, a dyslexic child, struggles in school due to undiagnosed learning difficulties. Labeled a problem child, he finds solace and support in a new art teacher, Nikhil, who recognizes Ishaan's artistic talent and helps him overcome his challenges. The film beautifully portrays the importance of understanding and supporting children with learning disabilities

inception

Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals information by entering people's dreams, is offered a chance to clear his criminal record by performing "inception," the act of planting an idea in the mind of a target during their dream state. Cobb assembles a team of specialists for a seemingly impossible heist that delves into the complexities of the human mind, dreams, and reality.
